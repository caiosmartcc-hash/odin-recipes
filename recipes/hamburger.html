Hamburger

Description

This hamburger recipe is flavorful, juicy, and incredibly satisfying. I'm sure you will love it!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Seasonings, herbs and sauces
  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Make four patties.
  3. Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center
